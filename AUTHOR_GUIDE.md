# Author Guide

This website can be updated from one main file:

[content/site.ts](/mnt/d/VS/edward%20website/content/site.ts)

If you do not want to edit code, just send these items and someone can update the site for you.

## What To Send

- Book title
- Short book description
- Buy link
- Author bio
- Reviews or endorsements
- Public contact email

## Before Publishing

Please check:

- The cover matches the book title
- The buy link goes to the correct book
- The bio is accurate
- The reviews are approved
- The contact email is correct
